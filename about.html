About Me

I’m Becky Cole, a developer in the making with a career full of creative detours that fuel everything I build. Before diving into code, I spent years in vocational education, designing courses and assessment tools that turned tricky subjects into engaging learning experiences.

I also ran an award-winning community radio station, where I produced shows, built audiences, and learned how to grab attention and keep it.

Now I’m channelling all of that into development.

Every project is a chance to blend an educator’s clarity, a media creator’s storytelling, and a coder’s precision to create apps and websites that truly connect with people. I thrive on new ideas, playful design, and clean, functional code.

I’m especially interested in building thoughtful digital products that feel warm, usable, and well-structured from the inside out.
